## Quick Decision Matrix

### When to Invest in Marketing
- **CAC < LTV/3**: Invest aggressively
- **CAC = LTV/3**: Maintain current spend
- **CAC > LTV/3**: Optimize channels

### When to Add Features
- **Feature requested by 20%+ of users**: High priority
- **Feature drives 10%+ conversion**: Implement
- **Feature reduces churn by 5%+**: Implement

### When to Adjust Pricing
- **Conversion rate >30%**: Consider raising prices
- **Conversion rate <10%**: Consider lowering prices
- **Churn rate >10%**: Improve value proposition
